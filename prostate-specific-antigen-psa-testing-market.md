<p><p>The Prostate Specific Antigen (PSA) testing market comprises various types of assays, predominantly focusing on Radioimmunoassay (RIA) and Enzyme Immunoassay (EIA). RIA employs radioactively labeled antibodies to quantify PSA levels, offering high sensitivity and specificity. Conversely, EIA utilizes enzyme-linked antibodies to detect PSA, providing a faster and more straightforward testing process. Both methods are crucial for prostate cancer screening and monitoring, facilitating early detection and treatment, thus significantly impacting patient outcomes and guiding clinical decisions.</p></p>
