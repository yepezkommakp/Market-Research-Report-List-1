<p><p>The Gynecology Electrosurgical Units (ESUs) market comprises several types: Monopole Electrosurgical Units, which use a single electrode for cutting and coagulation; Bipolar Electrosurgical Units, featuring two electrodes that localize energy to minimize thermal damage; and Vessel Sealing Units, designed specifically for precise coagulation and sealing of blood vessels. Each type serves distinct purposes in gynecological procedures, enhancing surgical precision, reducing blood loss, and improving patient outcomes through efficient tissue management and hemostasis.</p></p>
