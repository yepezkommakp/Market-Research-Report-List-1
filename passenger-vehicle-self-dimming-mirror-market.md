<p><p>The passenger vehicle self-dimming mirror market is divided into two main types: outer self-dimming mirrors and inside self-dimming mirrors. Outer self-dimming mirrors are installed on the exterior of the vehicle, automatically adjusting light intensity to reduce glare from trailing vehicles, enhancing nighttime driving safety. Inside self-dimming mirrors, typically mounted on the windshield, serve a similar purpose, minimizing glare from headlights behind the driver. Both types enhance visibility and driving comfort, contributing to overall vehicle safety features.</p></p>
