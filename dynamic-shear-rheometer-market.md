<p><strong>The Dynamic Shear Rheometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cone Plate Rheometer</li><li>Concentric Cylinder Rheometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Dynamic Shear Rheometer (DSR) market encompasses various types, primarily including cone plate and concentric cylinder rheometers. Cone plate rheometers utilize a cone-shaped tool placed over a flat plate, providing precise measurement of viscosity and shear properties, ideal for low-viscosity samples. Concentric cylinder rheometers consist of an outer cylinder and an inner spindle, allowing measurement of viscosity for high-viscosity materials. Both systems are essential for assessing the flow and deformation behavior of materials in diverse applications, including polymers, food, and coatings.</p></p>
