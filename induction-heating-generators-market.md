<p><p>Induction heating generators are categorized into medium frequency and high frequency generators, each serving distinct industrial applications. Medium frequency generators, typically operating between 1 kHz and 10 kHz, are ideal for processes like forging and heat treating, providing efficient heating for larger metal components. High frequency generators, ranging from 10 kHz to several MHz, are used for applications requiring precise control, such as soldering and electroplating, delivering rapid heating to smaller parts. Both types enhance efficiency and reduce energy consumption in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
