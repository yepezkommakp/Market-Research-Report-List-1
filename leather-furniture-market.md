<p><p>The leather furniture market is categorized into three main types: Faux Leather Furniture, Half Leather Furniture, and Whole Leather Furniture. Faux leather, made from synthetic materials, offers a budget-friendly and animal-friendly option with easy maintenance. Half leather combines genuine leather on seating areas with synthetic materials on the back and sides, striking a balance between cost and quality. Whole leather furniture, made entirely from genuine leather, is premium in quality, durability, and aesthetic appeal, catering to luxury consumers.</p></p>
