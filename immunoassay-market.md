<p><p>The immunoassay market encompasses various techniques for detecting and quantifying biomolecules. ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) is widely used for its sensitivity and versatility, while rapid tests provide quick results, often for point-of-care settings. Western blotting is used for protein analysis, offering specificity through protein separation. ELISPOT detects individual cell secretions, useful in immunology research. Immuno-PCR combines immunoassay and PCR for enhanced sensitivity. Other technologies include microfluidics and multiplex assays, broadening applications in diagnostics and research.</p></p>
