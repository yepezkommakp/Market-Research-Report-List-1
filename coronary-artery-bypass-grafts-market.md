<p><p>The Coronary Artery Bypass Grafts (CABG) market is segmented into three main types: Off-pump CABG, On-pump CABG, and Minimally Invasive Direct CABG (MIDCAB). Off-pump CABG involves performing the surgery without the use of a heart-lung machine, allowing for a less invasive approach that may reduce recovery time. On-pump CABG utilizes a heart-lung machine to maintain blood circulation during surgery, enabling access to all parts of the heart. MIDCAB is a minimally invasive technique targeting specific arteries, enhancing recovery and reducing hospital stay.</p></p>
