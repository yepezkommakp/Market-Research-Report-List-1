<p><p>Performance management software can be categorized into two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise solutions are installed locally on a company's servers, offering greater control and customization but requiring significant IT resources for maintenance and updates. In contrast, cloud-based solutions are hosted on remote servers, providing easy access and scalability while minimizing upfront costs. Cloud options typically feature automatic updates and lower IT demands, making them attractive for businesses seeking flexibility and efficiency in managing employee performance.</p></p>
