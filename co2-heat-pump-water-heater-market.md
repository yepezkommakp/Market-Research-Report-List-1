<p><p>The CO2 Heat Pump Water Heater market includes three main types: air source, water source, and other systems. Air source heat pumps extract heat from the ambient air, making them versatile and cost-effective for various climates. Water source heat pumps utilize heat from nearby water bodies, offering high efficiency but potentially limited by geographic availability. Other market types encompass innovative technologies, such as ground source systems, which draw heat from the ground, providing stable performance but requiring more installation space.</p></p>
