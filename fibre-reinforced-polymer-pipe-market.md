<p><p>Fibre Reinforced Polymer (FRP) pipe is a composite material made from a polymer matrix reinforced with fibrous materials, such as glass, carbon, or aramid fibers. FRP pipes are increasingly utilized in various industries, including oil and gas, wastewater treatment, and chemical processing, due to their lightweight nature and ease of installation.</p><p>The Fibre Reinforced Polymer Pipe Market is poised for significant growth, driven by factors such as increasing industrialization, rising demand for corrosion-resistant materials, and the need for cost-effective infrastructure solutions.
