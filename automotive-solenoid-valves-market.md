<p><p>The automotive solenoid valves market comprises various types, including 2-way, 3-way, 4-way, and 5-way valves, each serving distinct functions in vehicle systems. 2-way valves control the flow of fluid in a single direction, while 3-way valves manage the flow between two paths. 4-way valves enable reverse flow direction, crucial in systems like air suspension. 5-way valves offer additional routing options for more complex operations.
