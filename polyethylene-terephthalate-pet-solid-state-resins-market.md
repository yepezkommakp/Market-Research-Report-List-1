<p><p>The Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) solid-state resins market is primarily divided into two types: Conventional PET and Modified PET. Conventional PET is widely used for packaging applications due to its excellent strength, chemical resistance, and clarity. Modified PET, on the other hand, incorporates additives or modifications to enhance specific properties like impact resistance, thermal stability, or barrier performance, making it suitable for specialized applications. Together, these types cater to diverse industries, including food and beverage packaging, textiles, and automotive components.</p></p>
