<p><p>The canister-based avalanche airbags market primarily consists of two types: mono and dual avalanche airbags. Mono avalanche airbags deploy a single air chamber, providing basic protection and ease of use for individual riders in backcountry environments. In contrast, dual avalanche airbags feature two air chambers, offering enhanced stability and better lift during an avalanche. This dual system is particularly beneficial for those seeking advanced safety measures. Both types are essential tools for avalanche safety, catering to different user preferences and risk levels.</p></p>
