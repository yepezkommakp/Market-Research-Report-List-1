<p><p>The marking coatings market primarily consists of water-based and solvent-based marking paints. Water-based marking paints are eco-friendly, easy to clean, and emit fewer volatile organic compounds (VOCs), making them ideal for indoor applications and areas requiring low environmental impact. Conversely, solvent-based marking paints offer superior durability and adhesion, making them suitable for outdoor use and heavy-duty applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, including construction, road marking, and sports facilities, each chosen based on specific performance needs and environmental considerations.</p></p>
