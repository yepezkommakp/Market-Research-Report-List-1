<p><p>The liquid supersonic flowmeters market is primarily categorized into non-contact and contact supersonic flowmeters. Non-contact supersonic flowmeters measure fluid flow without direct interaction, using ultrasonic waves to gauge flow rates, making them ideal for applications requiring minimal disruption. In contrast, contact supersonic flowmeters involve direct contact with the liquid, providing precise measurements in various conditions. Both types are essential in industries such as oil and gas, chemicals, and water treatment, offering efficiency and accuracy in flow measurement.</p></p>
