<p><p>The capacitive displacement sensor market is primarily categorized into cylindrical and flat types. Cylindrical sensors are typically used in applications requiring measurement of radial displacement, offering a compact design suitable for tight spaces. Flat sensors, on the other hand, are designed for applications that necessitate surface measurement over a wider area, often providing higher precision and stability. Both types leverage changes in capacitance to detect displacement, finding utility in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing for precise measurement tasks.</p></p>
