<p><p>The automotive green glass market comprises primarily two types: Infra-red Cut and Ultraviolet Cut glass. Infra-red Cut glass is designed to block harmful infrared radiation, reducing heat build-up inside vehicles while enhancing passenger comfort and energy efficiency. On the other hand, Ultraviolet Cut glass effectively filters out harmful UV rays, protecting passengers and the vehicle's interior from sun damage. Both types contribute to improved energy management and passenger safety, while also addressing eco-friendly initiatives in the automotive industry.</p></p>
