<p><p>The semipermeable membrane market is primarily categorized into composite membranes and asymmetric membranes. Composite membranes consist of multiple layers, enhancing selectivity and permeability for various applications, including water treatment and gas separation. Asymmetric membranes feature a dense top layer and a porous support layer, offering efficiency in filtration processes while maintaining structural integrity. Both membrane types are crucial in sectors like pharmaceuticals, wastewater treatment, and desalination, driving advancements in separation technology and optimizing performance across various industries.</p></p>
