<p><p>The cryogenic freezer market is classified mainly into two types: Type I and Type II. Type I freezers utilize liquid nitrogen for rapid cooling and are mainly used in laboratory and medical applications to preserve biological samples. Type II freezers, on the other hand, employ advanced refrigeration technologies to maintain ultra-low temperatures, often catering to industrial and pharmaceutical sectors. Both types ensure effective preservation, but they serve different requirements based on the application's temperature control and storage duration needs.</p></p>
