<p><p>The X-Ray protective gloves market consists of two primary types: lead gloves and lead-free gloves. Lead gloves are made with a combination of rubber and lead shielding, offering high radiation protection, typically used in procedures requiring significant radiation exposure. In contrast, lead-free gloves utilize advanced materials like polymer composites providing lighter protection without heavy metals, appealing to users prioritizing comfort and mobility. Both options are crucial in medical and industrial settings to safeguard personnel from harmful radiation during X-Ray examinations.</p></p>
