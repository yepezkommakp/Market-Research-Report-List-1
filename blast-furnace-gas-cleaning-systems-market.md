<p><p>Blast furnace gas cleaning systems are essential for removing impurities from gases produced during iron and steel manufacturing. The market is divided into two main types: dry cleaning systems and wet cleaning systems. Dry cleaning systems utilize filters and cyclones to separate dust and particulates without using water, making them more energy-efficient. In contrast, wet cleaning systems employ scrubbing techniques with liquid solutions to capture pollutants, effectively reducing emissions but requiring more maintenance and operational resources. Both systems play crucial roles in meeting environmental standards.</p></p>
