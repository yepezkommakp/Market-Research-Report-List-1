<p><strong>The Hydrogen Refueling Station Chiller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air-cooled Chiller</li><li>Water-cooled Chiller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the Hydrogen Refueling Station Chiller market, there are two primary types: air-cooled chillers and water-cooled chillers. Air-cooled chillers utilize ambient air to dissipate heat, making them easier to install and maintain, but may be less efficient in high-temperature environments. In contrast, water-cooled chillers use water to absorb heat, offering higher efficiency and cooling capacity, especially in larger installations. Both types cater to the specific cooling needs of hydrogen refueling stations, ensuring optimal performance during the refueling process.</p></p>
