<p><strong>The Structural Steel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>I-Beam</li><li>Angle (L-Shape)</li><li>Hollow Structural Section (HSS) Shape</li><li>Z-Shape</li><li>T-Shaped</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The structural steel market features various shapes, each serving unique applications. I-beams are widely used for their strength in construction, supporting floors and roofs. Angle (L-shape) steel is versatile, often used for brackets and frameworks. Hollow Structural Sections (HSS) provide strength while being lightweight, making them ideal for modern designs. Z-shapes are commonly used in roof and wall applications, ensuring stability. T-shaped sections offer additional support in beams and frames, enhancing structural integrity across diverse projects.</p></p>
