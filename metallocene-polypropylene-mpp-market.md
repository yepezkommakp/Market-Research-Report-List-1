<p><p>Metallocene Polypropylene (mPP) is a type of polypropylene produced using metallocene catalysts, which enhances its properties. The mPP market is segmented into two main types: Metallocene Isotactic Polypropylene (miPP) and Metallocene Syndiotactic Polypropylene (msPP). miPP offers high clarity, impact strength, and thermal resistance, making it suitable for packaging and automotive applications. In contrast, msPP exhibits excellent dimensional stability and stiffness, making it ideal for applications like films and fibers, while also providing improved barrier properties.</p></p>
