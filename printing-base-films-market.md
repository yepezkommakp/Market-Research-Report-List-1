<p><ul><li>Biaxially Stretched Film</li><li>Unidirectional Stretched Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The printing base films market comprises two primary types: biaxially stretched films and unidirectional stretched films. Biaxially stretched films are produced by stretching the film in both machine and transverse directions, enhancing strength and clarity, making them ideal for high-quality printing applications. In contrast, unidirectional stretched films are stretched in one direction, offering specific characteristics suitable for particular uses. Both types cater to different industry needs, with biaxially stretched films typically used in packaging and labeling, while unidirectional films serve specialized applications.</p></p>
