<p><ul><li>Zinc Sulfate Monohydrate</li><li>Zinc Sulfate Heptahydrate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial grade zinc sulfate market primarily comprises two types: zinc sulfate monohydrate and zinc sulfate heptahydrate. Zinc sulfate monohydrate is characterized by a single molecule of water and is widely used in agriculture as a micronutrient for crops and in various industrial applications, including chemicals and pharmaceuticals. Zinc sulfate heptahydrate contains seven water molecules, making it more soluble, and is primarily utilized in fertilizers, livestock feed supplements, and water treatment processes. Both forms play essential roles in enhancing agricultural productivity and industrial processes.</p></p>
