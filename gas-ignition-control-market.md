<p><p>The gas ignition control market is categorized into three main types: Hot Surface Ignition (HSI) Type, which uses a heated element to ignite gas directly; Direct Spark Ignition (DSI) Type, utilizing an electrical spark to initiate combustion; and Intermittent Pilot Ignition (IP) Type, which employs a pilot flame that is ignited intermittently. Each type offers unique advantages in energy efficiency, safety, and application suitability, catering to various consumer needs in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
