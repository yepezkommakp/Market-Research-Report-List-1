<p><p>Strainers are essential components in fluid systems, serving to filter out debris and prevent damage to equipment. Y-type strainers feature a compact design suitable for limited space, effectively removing particles. Basket strainers have a larger filtering area, making them ideal for applications with higher flow rates. Duplex strainers allow for continuous operation, enabling one side to filter while the other is serviced. Other types include simplex strainers and magnetic strainers, each designed for specific filtration needs and applications.</p></p>
