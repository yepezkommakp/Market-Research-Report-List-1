<p><p>The Atomic Layer Etching (ALE) system market can be categorized into two main types: Plasma Type and High Temperature Type. Plasma Type systems utilize plasma to create reactive species, facilitating precise material removal at the atomic level, ideal for delicate structures. In contrast, High Temperature Type systems employ elevated temperatures to enhance reaction rates, allowing for efficient etching of materials that may be more resistant to lower temperatures. Both types cater to the growing demand for advanced semiconductor fabrication processes.</p></p>
