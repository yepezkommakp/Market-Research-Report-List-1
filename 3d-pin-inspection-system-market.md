<p><p>The 3D Pin Inspection System market is divided into two primary types: Contact Type and Contactless Type. Contact Type systems utilize physical probes to measure the dimensions and features of pins, providing precise and direct interaction with the pins being inspected. In contrast, Contactless Type systems employ advanced imaging technologies, such as lasers or cameras, to analyze pin geometry without any physical contact. Both types facilitate quality control in manufacturing, ensuring high accuracy and efficiency in production processes.</p></p>
