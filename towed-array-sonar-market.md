<p><p>The towed array sonar market encompasses two primary types: active and passive towed array sonar. Active towed array sonar emits sound waves to detect and localize objects underwater, providing real-time data on surrounding marine activity. Conversely, passive towed array sonar listens for sounds generated by external sources, such as submarines or marine life, without emitting its own signals. Both systems are crucial for naval operations, maritime security, and environmental monitoring, catering to distinct operational needs across various applications.</p></p>
