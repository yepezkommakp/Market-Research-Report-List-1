<p><p>Stamped concrete markets can be categorized into three main types. The addition of a base color involves integrating pigments into the concrete mix, providing a uniform appearance that enhances the overall aesthetic. The addition of an accent color is applied on top of the base color, typically in specific areas, to highlight patterns or features, creating visual contrast. Stamping a pattern into the concrete involves using specialized molds to create textured designs, mimicking materials like stone or tile, resulting in a more sophisticated finish.</p></p>
