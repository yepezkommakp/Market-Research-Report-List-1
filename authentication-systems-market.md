<p><p>The authentication systems market encompasses various types aimed at enhancing security. Single Factor Authentication (SFA) relies on one credential, typically a password, making it less secure. Two Factor Authentication (2FA) adds a second layer, such as a text message code, providing improved protection against unauthorized access. Multi Factor Authentication (MFA) goes further by employing two or more verification methods, which may include biometrics or hardware tokens, thereby offering robust security for sensitive data and applications in diverse sectors.</p></p>
