<p><p>The rotary tablet press market is primarily segmented into single rotary and double-sided rotary tablet presses. Single rotary tablet presses are designed with a single set of dies, facilitating the production of tablets on one side, making them suitable for lower output needs. In contrast, double-sided rotary tablet presses feature dies on both sides, allowing for higher production rates and efficiency, ideal for large-scale manufacturing. Both types are crucial in the pharmaceutical, food, and nutraceutical industries for tablet formulation.</p></p>
