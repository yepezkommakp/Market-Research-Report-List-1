<p><ul><li>Emulsion PCR</li><li>Bridge Amplification</li><li>Single-Molecule</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gene sequence detection market comprises several amplification techniques, including Emulsion PCR, Bridge Amplification, and Single-Molecule amplification. Emulsion PCR enables clonal amplification in a water-in-oil emulsion, providing high throughput and efficiency. Bridge Amplification is utilized in platforms like Illumina, creating dense clusters of DNA on a solid surface for sequencing. Single-Molecule amplification detects individual DNA molecules, allowing for accurate analysis without amplification bias, and is crucial for applications like single-cell genomics and rare variant detection. These methods cater to diverse research and clinical needs.</p></p>
