<p><strong>The Spray Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Water Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spray paint market is primarily divided into two types: solvent-based and water-based formulations. Solvent-based spray paints utilize organic solvents as their base, offering durable finishes and excellent adhesion, making them ideal for outdoor applications. However, they may emit strong fumes and require proper ventilation. In contrast, water-based spray paints use water as a solvent, resulting in lower VOC emissions and easier cleanup. They are suitable for indoor use and environmentally friendly, although their durability may be slightly less than that of solvent-based options.</p></p>
