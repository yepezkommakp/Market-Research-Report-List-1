<p><p>Flue-gas desulfurization (FGD) equipment is essential for reducing sulfur dioxide emissions from industrial processes. The market is divided into three main types: Wet FGD, which uses a chemical solution to scrub SO2 from flue gases, providing high efficiency; Dry FGD, which employs a dry reagent, generating less wastewater; and Semi-Dry FGD, combining elements of both methods by using a spray dryer to capture SO2 and produce a dry byproduct. Each type varies in cost, efficiency, and application suitability.</p></p>
